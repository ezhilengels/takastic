Overview

Taskastic ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal task management app. We built it to help you stay organised and get things done. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Taskastic mobile application, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Short version: We collect only what is necessary to run the app. We do not sell your data, show you ads, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

Data Why we collect it Where it's stored
Email address Account creation and authentication Supabase (encrypted at rest)
Password Account security Supabase — stored as a salted hash, never in plain text
Task data (title, priority, status, due date) Core app functionality — displaying and syncing your tasks Supabase + device local cache (AsyncStorage)
Push notification token Sending you task-due reminders Expo / device only — not stored on our servers

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, contacts, photos, payment information, or device identifiers beyond what Expo's SDK includes in its standard notification flow.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To sync your tasks across devices in real time
  • To send push notifications at the due time you set for a task
  • To restore your task list if you reinstall the app
  • To improve app stability and fix bugs (via error logs — no personal data included)

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase, a PostgreSQL-based cloud database hosted on AWS infrastructure. Supabase encrypts data at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Row-level security (RLS) policies ensure you can only read and write your own tasks — no other user can access your data.

A local cache of your tasks is stored on your device using AsyncStorage to allow offline access. This data is sandboxed to the Taskastic app and is not accessible to other apps on your device.

Offline mode: If you make changes while offline, they are queued locally and synced to the server when your connection is restored. Queued data never leaves your device until a successful sync occurs.

Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, Taskastic schedules reminders locally on your device at the exact due time of each task. We use Expo Notifications to manage this.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings app under Taskastic → Notifications. Disabling notifications does not affect your task data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in these limited cases:

  • Supabase — our database provider, acting as a data processor under a Data Processing Agreement. They process data only as instructed by us.
  • Expo — for push notification delivery. Only an anonymous device token is shared, never your email or task content.
  • Legal requirement — if required by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority.

Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your tasks are visible to you at all times within the app.
  • Edit: You can edit or delete any individual task at any time.
  • Delete your account: Use our Account Deletion Request page to permanently delete your account and all associated data. We will action this within 30 days and confirm by email.
  • Export: Contact us to request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Notifications: Opt out at any time via your device Settings.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data (email, tasks) is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated analytics (if any) that cannot be tied back to you may be retained indefinitely.

Children's Privacy

Taskastic is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

For significant changes, we will notify you via a notice in the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
