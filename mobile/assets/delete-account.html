Delete Your Account

Submit a request below to permanently delete your Taskastic account and all associated data. We will process your request within 30 days.

⚠️
This action is permanent and cannot be undone

Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please make sure you no longer need access to your tasks before proceeding.

What will be deleted

  • 👤
    Your account
    Email address, password, and authentication data
  • ✅
    All your tasks
    Every task you have created, including completed ones
  • ☁️
    Synced data
    All data stored on our servers (Supabase)
  • 🔔
    Notification preferences
    All scheduled reminders will be cancelled

What happens after you submit

1
Request received
You'll get a confirmation email within 24 hours acknowledging your request.
2
Data deleted
Your account and all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
3
Confirmation sent
We'll email you once deletion is complete.
